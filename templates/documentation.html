Welcome to the Crypto Hide documentation. Below are guides to help you use our features:

1. How to Encrypt Messages in Images

  • Step 1: Upload Your Image
  • 1. Click on the "Upload Image" button.
  • 2. Choose the image file from your device that you want to use.
  • 3. Click the "Upload" button to proceed.
  • Step 2: Enter Your Message
  • 1. In the "Enter your message..." text area, type the message you want to encrypt within the image.
  • Step 3: Encrypt Your Message
  • 1. Click the "Encrypt" button to start the encryption process.
  • Step 4: View Encrypted Image
  • 1. Once the encryption is complete, an encrypted image will be generated.
  • 2. The encrypted image will be displayed on the page.
  • Step 5: Download Encrypted Image
  • 1. To save the encrypted image, click the "Download Encrypted Image" link.
  • 2. The encrypted image will be downloaded to your device.

2. How to Decrypt Messages from an Encrypted Image

  • Step 1: Choose Encrypted Image
  • 1. If you have an encrypted image, click on the "Choose Encrypted Image" button.
  • 2. Select the encrypted image file from your device.
  • Step 2: Decrypt Message
  • 3. Click the "Decrypt" button to start the decryption process.
  • Step 3: View Decrypted Message
  • 1. Once the decryption is complete, the decrypted message will be revealed.
  • 2. The decrypted message will be displayed on the page.
